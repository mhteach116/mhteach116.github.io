About This Lesson


This lesson will focus on students who wish to learn English for business or work related reasons.


  • Business English vocabulary.
  • Helping student prepare for overseas job interviews.
  • Improving students presentation skills.
  • Improving students English for making phone calls and meetings.

Booking Overview

There are 4 types of lessons: Conversation, Reading, Business and Kids.


Lessons are either or 30 or 60 minutes long.


Save 10% when you book 4 lessons at the same time.

Save 15% when you book 8 lessons at the same time.


Once you have chosen the desired time length and lesson pack, you will be then redirected to PayPal to make a payment. After that you will be redirected back to MH Teach to book your time and date for your chosen lesson pack.


If you have purchased multiple lesson packs: “Multiple” or “Regular”, you will be able to book the time and date for your first lesson only. For the remaining lessons, we will contact you by email with calendar booking so that you can book the rest of the lessons when it is convenient for you. You will have a total of 6 months to use your remaining lessons from the purchased pack.


Payments are accepted by PayPal only. You can pay for the lessons with your credit or debit card without having to create an account, however MH Teach suggests new students to make a PayPal account so it can be easier to make future payments.


MH Teach uses Zoom, Google Meet or Skype for lessons. Please inform the teacher what platform is convenient for you to take lessons from or feel free to learn the basics of the ideal platform.
