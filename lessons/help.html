Types of Lessons

Conversation


This lesson will focus on improving the students' conversation skills and improving their accent. The goal of this lesson is to improve the students everyday conversation ability.


  • Will focus on improving accent.
  • Vocabulary for everyday English.
  • Roleplay activities for the student to practice English in different scenarios.
  • Students can practice talking in English in whatever subject they like.


Reading


This lesson will focus on reading. The teacher will have reading material ready for the student or using the student’s preference of reading material. The student can ask any questions to the teacher at any time. The goal of this lesson is to improve the student’s reading ability.


  • Students can request custom reading material.
  • Reading assistance.
  • Explaining the meaning of the reading material
  • Answering any questions at any time to help the student read better


Business


This lesson will focus on students who wish to learn English for business or work related reasons.


  • Business English vocabulary.
  • Helping student prepare for overseas job interviews.
  • Improving students presentation skills.
  • Improving students English for making phone calls and meetings.


Kids


This is a special lesson that is designed for kids from age 5 onwards and this lesson will help your child speak British English from a young age which will help them to develop a British accent later in their life. This lesson is a combination of reading, conversation and activities that will help your child speak more and they can interact with the teacher.


Please kindly be near your child when they are learning online due to health and safety reasons.


  • Your child can develop a British English accent.
  • This lesson will be a mix of reading and conversation.
  • Your child can be more confident to engage in conversation with foreigners.
  • Your child can learn to be bilingual.


Lesson Structure


Whatever the type of the lesson it is, students do not have to worry about preparing material for the class since the teacher will have the courseware ready for the students to learn from.


Every lesson will be designed to get the most out of a student’s learning. New vocabulary, pronunciation, grammar and accent improvement will be taught. Mistakes and errors will be recorded and after the end of each lesson, feedback will be provided to the student via email. Homework can be assigned, if the student wishes so.
